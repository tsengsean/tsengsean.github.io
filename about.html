About

Hi! I'm Sean Tseng, and I'm a storyteller.

For the past twelve years, I've immersed myself in one of the richest mediums of storytelling: language. From creative writing clubs and classes to majors centered around literature and the science of language itself, I've done all I can to explore this basic building block of storytelling. However, I've recently found that a crucial aspect of storytelling has fallen away from my notice in those twelve years——imagery. Now, by combining my love for design, visual arts, and language, I hope to tell the best story I can, in a way that only I can.
