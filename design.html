CMYK Designathon Wall Mock-Up

My team and I placed 3rd with our design for 1951's Community Wall ask. We chose to focus on the visualization and reinforcement of community with our design while also capitalizing on the interactivity and virality of photo opportunities in our visual world.

Abstract Typewriter

As part of our midterm project for a prototyping and fabrication course, my team and I designed and prototyped a working mechanism that imitates the sound of typewriters.

Timekeyper App Mock-Up

In a product management exercise, I made a mock-up for an app to help students in their transition between highschool and college by offering a dynamic, time-budgeting smart scheduler.

Self-Branding Exercise

During a self-branding assignment for my Photoshop and Illustrator course, I made the first iteration of what has become my logo—a symbol made out of my initials that can be read left to right for my Chinese name and right to left for my English name.
